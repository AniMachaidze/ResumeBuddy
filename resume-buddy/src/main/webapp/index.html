ResumeBuddy

What it is?

ResumeBuddy is a webapp that helps people get & provide feedback on each other's resumes. Currently, people may not have access to good quality reviewers for their resumes. If someone is looking for review from someone already working in their field of interest, they can use our web app to be connected to one! ResumeBuddy offers a free virtual professional development service to anyone who may not otherwise have access to it.

Who do we build for?

ResumeBuddy is for anyone seeking critique and tips from qualified reviewers to help improve their resume. It is also for people who want to volunteer to help review resumes and give back to the job seeking community.
